Why Moradabad?

Why is Moradabad the ultimate destination for brass and metal sourcing?

Moradabad, also known as the "Brass City of India," is the ultimate destination for brass sourcing and metal sourcing due to several key factors.

  • Cost-Effective Production Due to the availability of skilled labor and locally sourced raw materials, Moradabad offers competitive pricing without compromising on quality
  • Strong Export NetworkMoradabad exports brassware to countries like the UK and the Middle East. The city has a well-established supply chain and global trade.
  • Skilled Artisans: This expertise ensures high-quality workmanship and intricate designs in brass products.
  • Eco-Friendly ManufacturingMany manufacturers are adopting lead-free, recycled brass, and sustainable practices, making Moradabad a preferred choice for environmentally conscious buyers.
